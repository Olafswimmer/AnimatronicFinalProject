Disco Yeti and the Carboard Caballeros

An Exploration of Broken and Malfuntioning Animatronics at the Disney Theme Parks

Animatronics are a staple piece of technology at Disney's theme parks. From tiny crabs in Pirates of the Carribean, to a 45-foot-tall dragon in Fantasmic!, animatronics are present in practically every Disney attraction. But, since they are technology, all animatronics are bound to break from time to time. Most malfunctions are an easy fix, but others have such complex and expensive issues, that they remain broken to this day.
