Disco Yeti and the Cardboard Caballeros

An Exploration of Broken and Malfunctioning Animatronics and Attractions at the Disney Theme Parks

Audio-animatronics are a staple piece of technology at Disney's theme parks. From miniscule frogs in Splash Mountain, to a 45-foot-tall dragon in Fantasmic!, animatronics are present in practically every Disney attraction. But, since they are technology, all animatronics are bound to break from time to time. Most malfunctions are easily fixed, but others have such complex and expensive issues, that they remain broken for years... or until the attraction ceases to exist.
